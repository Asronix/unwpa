Tree Plantation ( The World Environmental Day)


10th of June 2021, in the supervision of UNWPA Bangladesh President H. E. Ratan Kumar Das , UNWPA Bangladesh has arranged a day long tree plantation program on the occasion of ” The World Environmental Day”. Today some Ambassadors of UNWPA Bangladesh chapter’s has plant some trees of Middle Badda area in Dhaka. They have planted different types of trees like as Herb tress, fruit trees, flowering plants etc.
